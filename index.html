Biking

Within mountain biking, I focus on the disciplines of Enduro and Cross Country racing. But I am always searching for ways to improve my riding skills and this includes diving into the disciplines of downhill and cycle-cross. Further more, I work with Joel Harwood and Blueprint Athlete Development to help improve my fitness, bike (handling) skills and race preparation.As for racing, my family ventures throughout Canada to partake in the Norco Canadian National Enduro Series (NCES) and Canada Cup cross-county (XCO) events; I have been participating in the NCES races for the past 5 years and XCO races for 3 years.

...My "short term" mountain biking goal is to race on a national level across many different disciplines; this includes Enduro and XCO races, along with select downhill and cycle-cross races. My "long term" goal is to race at a World Cup level.

Skiing

Within the ski racing, I race slalom, giant slalom and super-G disciplines in the U16 category. I am a member of the Lake Louise Ski Club and I have been in the race program for 8 years/seasons. I follow the local Alberta series races as well as national championships and western Canada based races.

...My ski racing goals are to race into FIS and at a national level. While having fun and enjoying all aspects of skiing; including freeskiing.
